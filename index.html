Our Mission

Finding ways to be the bridge between those in need and those that can help.

We work with Board Members across the United states to ensure your needs are met.

We want to let your needs to be front and center and will work our hardest with your best interest in mind.

How we help you!

Create

You can create a ticket detailing the issue you would like us to investigate. You can detail the issue type, and the urgency of how quickly it will need to be resolved.

Investigate

From the information received we will investigate your issue in order to find the best possible solution. We will keep in touch with you to give you updates to that you are always involved in the process.

Resolve

Once a resolution has been found we will inform you right away and ensure that everything is functioning the way you intended. Feel free to reach out if further assistance is need.
